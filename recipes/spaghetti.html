Spaghetti

Description

A classic spaghetti dish with a rich tomato meat sauce.

Simple, filling, and perfect for family dinners.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 ounces marinara sauce
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package directions.
  2. Brown the beef with the onion and garlic. Drain excess grease.
  3. Stir in the marinara sauce and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Serve the sauce over the spaghetti and top with Parmesan cheese.
