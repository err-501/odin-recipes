Pancakes

Description

Light and fluffy homemade pancakes.

Perfect for breakfast with butter and maple syrup.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Steps

  1. Mix the dry ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Whisk the milk, egg, and melted butter together.
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until just mixed.
  4. Cook on a lightly greased skillet until golden brown on both sides.
